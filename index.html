About Kathmandu University

Kathmandu University (KU) is an autonomous, not-for-profit, self-funding public institution. It is an institution of higher learning dedicated to maintaining the standard of academic excellence in various classical and professional disciplines.

  • Mission: To provide quality education for leadership.
  • Vision: To become a world-class university devoted to bringing knowledge and technology to the service of mankind.

Utility Management

Providing good and managed services in Kathmandu University is the main goal of the Mangement Team here. Utilites in Kathmandu University are also managed through Web Application: Kathmandu Unviersity Utility Mangement System

Our Services

Web Map

The users can view Web Map of Kathmandu University. They also have choice to switch between various base maps.

Query

New to Web App? No problem. Users can query their concerns about utilities in our system.

Area and Distance Measurement

Users can measure distance from one block to the other, or find area of the Football ground, and many more through Area and Distance Measurement.

Utility Concerns

Users can view their desired utility layer in this application. Electric poles, transmission line, roads, sewer, drainage, ...

Layers Toggling

Want to view your desired layer only? It's possible to toggle between various layers and basemaps here.

Complaint Registration

Can't withstand seeing something damaged? Immediately register the complaint here.

Management

The main objective of this application is to help users explore Kathmandu University, provide them detailed information of the wherabouts, and record and maintain the utilities around the University.
